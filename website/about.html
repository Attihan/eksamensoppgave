Climbing a Wall!

The OTS Klatregruppa (Climbing Group) at P35, OsloMet University, provides a low-threshold climbing experience for students. On Tuesdays, climbing sessions are open to those with brattkort/ledkort (climbing certifications) and students who are seeking partners or simply wish to climb alongside fellow enthusiasts. The climbing wall is accessible to all OsloMet students, and equipment including shoes, harnesses, ropes, belay devices, rappel devices, chalk bags, crash pads, and quickdraws can be borrowed, making it easy for students to get involved and enjoy the thrill of climbing.
