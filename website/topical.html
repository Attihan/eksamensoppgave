Benefits of being a student at OsloMET

Possibility to rent student housing

When you become a student at OsloMET, you are offered the opportunity to rent student housing in Oslo. On this website, you can find information about locations and the characteristics of their rooms, along with the prices.

Studentsamskipnaden i Oslo/SIO. Photo: https://bolig.sio.no/en/

Discounted membership at SIO Athletica

The current price for membership at SIO Athletica is 240 NOK per month when you are a student at OsloMET. Athletica has 6 centers in Oslo, which you can view on this link. In addition to this, there are many sports organizations for students, including, for example, OSI (Oslo Student Sports Club) with activities such as OSI Football, OSI Basketball, OSI Group Dance, and OSI Fencing. Beyond OSI, there are many other organizations not affiliated with OSI that you can participate in. Most organizations are beginner-friendly or have suitable level divisions. An overview of all sports organizations can be found on the SIO website.

Furthermore, you don't necessarily need to be a member of a student organization to find a group to train with. Athletica offers various types of group classes, from different types of dance to various types of yoga. Additionally, there are various cardio and strength training classes offered. Moreover, Athletica also offers classes in muay thai, jiu-jitsu, and boxing for those interested in martial arts. If you want a link to the page where you can get an overview of all types of classes and their schedules, here is the link to classes and schedules. You can also check if the classes are full; it's often advisable to try to book 5-7 days in advance, as some classes are popular.

Student Discounts

As a student at OsloMET, you often find student discounts on various services, such as public transport. Further information about a large number of discounts can be found through Studentorget. By clicking on this link, it will redirect you to the Studentorget website. Also, remember that if you are using a slightly more expensive service, there is never any harm in asking if they offer a student discount.
